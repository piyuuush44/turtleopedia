About Us

We are a team of Engineering and Management professionals of more than 5 years of experience in the corporate world. Over the course of learning new technologies or understanding the concepts, we found a gap of a not clear explanation of problems one faces nor relating the theories with real-life examples. Thus, we came upon an idea of building a blog where we can explain concepts and issues in a more personalized manner, which can help us prepare for our future and others too.

Why Turtleopedia

We drive inspiration from the famous rabbit and tortoise race where the slow and steady wins the race, but the real essence of the winning was the tortoise being focused and equipped with all gears to fight through the challenge. Thus, we do not want our audience to be slow. We want our audience to be ready with all the knowledge under their arsenal (the shell) and fight their way to the challenges and understand things steadily so that it stays with them for the long run.
